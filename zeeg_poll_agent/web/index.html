Poll Availability Finder

Paste a meeting-poll link. It lists the slots with no conflicts on your connected calendars, so you can fill the poll yourself.

Open poll ↗
Free / busy comes from Zeeg’s availability for the chosen scheduling page, which already accounts for every connected calendar (Google, Outlook, …). A slot is only marked free if Zeeg offers a bookable start at exactly that time; off-grid poll times (e.g. 9:10 on a page that books on the half-hour) are conservatively listed under conflicts — pick a finer-grained page to avoid this. This tool only reads; it never submits on your behalf.
